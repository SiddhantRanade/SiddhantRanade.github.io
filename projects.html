PSpice to eSim schematic converter

eSim (previously known as Oscad, FreeEDA) is an open source EDA tool for circuit design, simulation, analysis and PCB design. It is a free and open-source alternative to OrCAD® PSpice®. This converter tool allows you to convert existing PSpice projects to eSim projects. There is also an online version of the tool.

Course Project: CS-663: Digital Image Processing

This is an implementation of the paper Digital Photography with Flash and No-Flash Image Pairs in MATLAB. We have implemented the Trilateral Filter (in MATLAB) as well.
