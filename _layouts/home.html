About me

I am a PhD student in the School of Computing at the University of Utah, advised by Prof. Srikumar Ramalingam.

My research focuses on Computer Vision, particularly scene reconstruction.

I earned my Bachelors degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, where I majored in Engineering Physics, with a minor in Computer Science. At IITB, I worked with Prof. Siddhartha Chaudhuri on my Bachelors Thesis titled Material Prediction for Untextured 3D Models from 2D Images.
